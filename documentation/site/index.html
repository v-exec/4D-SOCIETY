4D SOCIETY is a first person exploration game with navigational puzzles driven by experimental graphics.

Realtime rendering has grown considerably in the past few decades. Our contemporary rendering techniques have served great purpose in making real-time rendering extremely efficient, and beautiful effects are sprinkled throughout the games we all love as a result of these advances. With that being said, few games commit to using strange graphics as a core mechanic of their gameplay. Nowadays, these graphics are limited to demoscene shows, esoteric shaders online, interactive art galleries, and VJs. 4D SOCIETY aims to bring these unusual and beautiful visuals to an interactive environment.

Inspired by games like Memories of a Broken Dimension, Fugue In Void, and NaissanceE, 4D SOCIETY explores environments filled with large-scale brutalist architecture, disorienting and experimental graphics, and a narrative with questions surrounding philosophical ideology.
